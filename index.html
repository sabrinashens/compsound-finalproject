WAVEFORM:

Required: Root+Chord+Scale

E.g., C9, Cm7, Cdim5, Caug3

Optional: Beats@Root+Chord+Scale'Inversion[Loop]

E.g., 2@C9, Cm7'1, Cdim5[2], 4@Caug3'2[3]

Chords are separated by space

=> C9 Cm7 Cdim5 Caug3

=> 2@C9 Cm7'1 Cdim5[2] 4@Caug3'2[3]

❒ Chord: \ , m, dim, aug

major, minor, diminished, augmented


❒ Scale: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9

(root),

(root, 3rd),

(root, 3rd, 5th, 7th),

(root, 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th)
